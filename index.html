What is CrispPop?

Free, one-tap audio repair for phone-shot video — fixes muddy, echoey, harsh, or quiet sound and bakes it straight back into your clip, still in sync. 🔒 100% private: everything runs in your browser; your video never uploads.

Under the candy, each preset drives a real 14-stage studio chain — de-clip, de-noise, de-reverb, and gate up front, then corrective EQ, compression, split-band de-essing, saturation, and platform-matched loudness. Flip to Studio mode to drive every stage yourself. (Restoration runs on our in-browser engine v1 — a deeper native engine is in the works.)

CrispPop is an indie project in open beta, tuned by a working musician — your first 5 saves are free on everything (free saves carry a tiny CrispPop badge; Pro takes it off). Found a bug or want a feature? Tell us in 60 seconds — every response is read by the person who builds this, and it decides what ships next.
